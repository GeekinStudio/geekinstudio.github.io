Geekin studio is a geek team set up on the basis of the Hangzhou No.14 Middle School,founded by a Ye Hongchen and Jiang Linzhi in May 2015.

Geekin specializes in handiwork, photography, graphic design, programming,literary creations and video clips.
Geekin does not have a long history,but the works must be fantastic.
Geekin not only co-produced several video clips with the Students Union,but also made posters for multiple activities.
Geekin maintains cooperating relations of many sides in current.
Geekin is an organization of geeks.Geek is not a narrow, but generalized definition,as long as you have expertise in a way can be geeks.
Come to join us!
For geeks,by geeks,we are geeks!
Prosperity perishes to the fullest,opening a new chapter of history.
